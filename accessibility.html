Accessibility Statement

Northmind Studies is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users, including individuals with disabilities. We strive to provide a website that is accessible and easy to use for everyone.

We aim to follow best practices and standards based on the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 Level AA.

Our website includes features such as keyboard navigation support, clear structure and headings, and compatibility with assistive technologies.

While we strive to adhere to these standards, some areas of the website may not yet fully meet accessibility guidelines. We are continuously working to improve accessibility over time.

If you experience any difficulty accessing content on this site or have suggestions for improvement, we welcome your feedback.
